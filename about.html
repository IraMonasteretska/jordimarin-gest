Jordi Marín Gest

Jordi Marín Gest

Born in Mexico City of French/Spanish parents, Jordi Marin-Gest began his career in figurative art, working in portraiture and sculpture. Having completed a BFA in Illustration in Laguna Beach, California, and a MFA in Sevilla, Spain, since 1996 he has exhibited in mixed disciplines of art, including shows in the USA, France, Spain, China, Germany and Mexico

"The fundamental source of creating art is life and living. Exploring the visual world and the creations held within it is my living goal, manifested within the graphic expression of my experience as a human being. My artistic focus is capturing a personal vision of the human condition, channeled through the mixture of physical painting and painting in 3D in Virtual Reality."
